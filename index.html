The Akan Name Generator

The Akan people of Ghana frequently name their childern after the day of the week they were born and their gender.
Here are the Ghanian day names:

Day of the week Sunday Monday Tuesday Wednesday Thursday Friday Saturday
Male Name Kwasi Kwadwo Kwabena Kwaku Yaw Kofi Kwame
Female name Akosua Adwoa Abenaa Akua Yaa Afua Ama
Here's a web application that takes a user's birthday and calculates the day of the week they were born and then depending on their gender and outputs their Akan Name.
