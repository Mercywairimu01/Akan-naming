The Akan Name Generator

The Akan people of Ghana frequently name their childern after the day of the week they were born and their gender.
Here are the Ghanian day names:

Day of the week Male Name Female name
Sunday Kwasi Akosua
Monday Kwadwo Adwoa
Tuesday Kwabena Abenaa
Wednesday Kwaku Akua
Thursday Yaw Yaa
Friday Kofi Afua
Saturday Kwame Ama

Here's a web application that takes a user's birthday and calculates the day of the week they were born and then depending on their gender and outputs their Akan Name. Try it out!!
